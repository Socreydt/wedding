*** Speaker Heak Island, Lake George ***

Explore

Lake George is one of our favorite places in the world, and we are thrilled to be able to share it with you! We will be shuttling people to and from the island from Dunham's Bay.

On the island we will have a picnic area, a wonderful spot to wade and swim, and access to a boat for tubing and skiing. On the island we will have snacks and sub sandwiches for you to enjoy. Please BYOB (we will have cups, coolers and ice).

The Sagamore

This greasy spoon is a local favorite. If you are out late, this place opens before the bars close! Check out their yelp page.

Martha's Icecream

Delicious coffee and awesome bagels. See the menu.

Rent a boat

Need a quick lunch? Sam makes sure he visits at least once every time he is in Saratoga. His favorite is the chicken caesar wrap… are you surprised?

Factory Outlets

Forgot to pack something? Looking for a new swim suit? Check out the many Premium Outlets just 20 minutes north of Saratoga Springs.

Lake George Village

It's beautiful and worth the 30 minute drive from Saratoga Springs. Check out Million Dollar Beach, rent a boat, and visit the iconic The Sagamore.

Hiking

Buck Mountain (5 miles, moderate), Black Mountain (6.2 miles, moderate), Pilot Knob Point (1.5 miles, easy to moderate), Shelving Rock Mountain (2.5 miles, easy)
