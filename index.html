Explore

We encourage you to check out Saratoga Springs, Lake George and the Adirondacks while you are here. There is so much to do! If you need more ideas, contact us. Click on the highlighted text for more information.

Compton's

This greasy spoon is a local favorite. If you are out late, this place opens before the bars close!

Uncommon Grounds

Delicious coffee and awesome bagels.

Spring Street Deli

Need a quick lunch? Sam makes sure he visits at least once every time he is in Saratoga. His favorite is the chicken caesar wrap… are you surprised?

Factory Outlets

Forgot to pack something? Looking for a new swim suit? Check out the many Premium Outlets just 20 minutes north of Saratoga Springs.

Lake George

It's beautiful and worth the 30 minute drive from Saratoga Springs. Check out Million Dollar Beach, rent a boat, and visit the iconic The Sagamore.

Hiking

Buck Mountain (5 miles, moderate), Black Mountain (6.2 miles, moderate), Pilot Knob Point (1.5 miles, easy to moderate), Shelving Rock Mountain (2.5 miles, easy)
